FreeBayes ↔ bcftools parameters (SNP-only)

- Base quality: FreeBayes --min-base-quality 13 (-q 13)  ↔  bcftools mpileup --min-BQ 13
- Mapping quality: FreeBayes --min-mapping-quality 20 (-m 20)  ↔  bcftools mpileup --min-MQ 20
- Coverage (depth): FreeBayes --min-coverage 8  ↔  bcftools filter 'sum(INFO/DP4)>=8' (DP4=REF_fwd,REF_rev,ALT_fwd,ALT_rev)
- Alt read count: FreeBayes --min-alternate-count 5 (-C 5)  ↔  bcftools filter '(INFO/DP4[2]+INFO/DP4[3])>=5'
- Allele fraction: FreeBayes --min-alternate-fraction 0.2 (-F 0.2)  ↔  bcftools filter '(INFO/DP4[2]+INFO/DP4[3])/sum(INFO/DP4)>=0.2'
- SNP-only: enforced via FreeBayes -i -X -u and bcftools view -v snps
- Normalization: 'bcftools norm -f REF' on both

Notes on enforcement in this repo
- bcftools: thresholds are applied via mpileup flags (BQ/MQ) and DP4‑based filters.
- FreeBayes: thresholds are enforced directly in FreeBayes using flags:
  - --min-base-quality 13 (-q 13)
  - --min-mapping-quality 20 (-m 20)
  - --min-coverage 8
  - --min-alternate-count 5 (-C 5)
  - --min-alternate-fraction 0.2 (-F 0.2)
  - SNP-only via: -i -X -u (ignore indels, MNPs, complex)
  - Normalization of output is done with 'bcftools norm'.
